General Intructions

Ensure your internet connection is stable.
Check that your computer’s audio is working.
Test your computer’s webcam.
Dress professionally and avoid bright colors.
Find a quiet, private, well-lit place.
Place should be free from possible interruptions.

How to record an effective video profile

An effective video profile includes:

  • Quick introduction of yourself and what you are looking for
  • A very brief example of your experience and the current / last position
  • Highlight what makes you stand out
  • What makes you unique/What's your USP?
  • Close with a strong statement

Once you are ready make sure you have everything set up and ready.

  • Lighting – your face should be clearly visible
  • Camera angle – you should directly look at the camera while speaking.
  • Pay attention to the background of shots: make sure it looks tidy and that there are no noises in the background.
  • Test your camera and microphone before you start recording.
  • Dress appropriately
  • Ensure that you are in a quite place without any distractions

Record the video profile and make sure

  • To keep it within 1 min
  • Talk slowly and clearly
  • Keep eye contact with the camera
  • Be creative
  • Highlight key achievements and skills
  • Show confidence and enthusiasm
  • Leave a positive impression with a powerful closing statement
